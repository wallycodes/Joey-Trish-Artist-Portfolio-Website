About

Joey is a UK based designer, specialising in Branding, Graphic/Web Design, Illustration & Photography. He has a worldwide client roster and his work is often featured in design related publications. Mike is also a regular speaker at design/tech conferences.

Get in touch: joey@joeytrishus.com

Bigeye

I recently had the privilege of working with Orlando based advertising agency, Bigeye. From a new logo mark to a new colour palette, typography, graphic design, illustration, messaging, website, we did it all. After more than fifteen years in the business, Bigeye has a huge reputation. The rebrand was created to better convey the dynamism of a company that has grown, not only in size but in the quality and range of work it's able to produce. Bigeye is a world-class agency and we designed a brand to reflect this.

Date: October 2019
Client: Bigeye
Role: Designer
URL: bigeyeagency.com

Get in touch: joey@joeytrishus.com

GoodDollar

I was lucky enough to be asked Tal Oran and Etoro to create the branding and website design for GoodDollar. The GoodDollar experiment is a series of tests with the ultimate purpose of reducing wealth inequality through blockchain technology. Their mission is to build a new, global, open-source cryptocurrency – called GoodDollar – to distribute money using the principles of universal basic income (UBI).

Date: 11.11.2018
Client: Etoro
Role: Art Director / Designer
URL: gooddollar.org
